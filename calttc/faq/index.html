FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

"I'm interested! When can I come play?"
Please check our Open Rec schedule and join our Facebook group for updates everyday.

"Where are you located?"
We play at the back of the basketball court at the back of the first floor of the RSF.

"What is the age limit?"
There is no age limit.

"How good do you have to join the club?"
Anyone can join! The club ranges from beginner to higher level players.

"Is there any way I can drop in for one day just to get a feel of whether to commit?"
Yes, ask for a sign in sheet when you arrive and you are all set.

"What is the general level of players in this club?"
The level of the club is pretty diverse. The actual team that competes is about 2000-2500, but there is Open Rec for recreational players as well. The team plays in divisionals, regionals and nationals, which are all really fun and good experiences.
If you are interested, email us at calttc@gmail.com and we can set up a date to practice/get a feel of your level.

"Is there a membership fee?"
Yes. Membership fees generally range from $20-$25 per semester. This includes access to tables, balls, and paddles provided.

"Where can I buy a ping pong paddle/balls?"
You can find good quality paddles for cheap here.
For more serious players, Joola and Butterfly are good alternatives

"Are there tournaments or round robins I can participate in?"
Yes! We have NCTTA tournaments as well as USATT sanctioned tournaments. USATT tournaments are typically held twice a year. For those of you who do not want to compete in tournaments and play for fun, the club hosts a weekly round robin on Saturday at 5PM.
Check the Facebook group for time and updates.
